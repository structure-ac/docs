# People

People are at the center of Structure. On this page, we'll dive into hoew you can use the person endpoint to enrich a linked url programmatically. {{ className: 'lead' }}

## The Person model

The person model contains all the information about the person from linked in and other sources.

### Properties

<Properties>
  <Property name="key" type="string">
    Unique identifier for the person.
  </Property>
  <Property name="awards" type="array">
    Awards for the person.
  </Property>
  <Property name="skills" type="array">
    A persons listed skills.
  </Property>
  <Property name="courses" type="array">
    Courses a person has listed.
  </Property>
  <Property name="patents" type="array">
    Patents a person has listed.
  </Property>
  <Property name="premium" type="boolean">
    A person's premium linkedin status.
  </Property>
</Properties>
